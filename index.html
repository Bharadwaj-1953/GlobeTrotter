GlobeTrotter

Plan Smart. Travel Better.

About the Project

GlobeTrotter is a travel planning system designed for college students. It helps users search, compare, and plan trips without switching between multiple platforms. The system supports budget-based planning, comparison of travel options, and itinerary management.

User Research

We conducted semi-structured interviews with six college students to understand how they plan trips and what challenges they face. The study showed that students struggle with limited budgets, time constraints, and group coordination.

Key Problems

  • Switching between multiple travel platforms
  • Slow group decision-making
  • Planning feels stressful and time-consuming

User Needs

  • Compare options in one place
  • Clear cost and time information
  • Better support for group planning
  • Simpler and faster workflows

Storyboards

Simple Task – Budget Trip Planning

A student searches for travel options within a fixed budget, compares available plans, and saves the best option.

Moderate Task – Comparing Travel Options

Users compare multiple travel options and evaluate trade-offs between cost and travel time before making a decision.

Complex Task – Itinerary and Events

Users explore nearby events, compare options, and add selected activities to their itinerary for a complete travel plan.
