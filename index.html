Welcome to the GlobeTrotter Project!

About Our Project

GlobeTrotter is a student-centered travel planning platform designed to make trip planning easier for college students. The project focuses on helping users compare travel options, manage budget limits, reduce planning stress, and coordinate decisions more easily when traveling alone or with a group.

Our goal is to bring important planning steps into one place so users do not need to switch between multiple websites to compare flights, hotels, transportation, and activities. GlobeTrotter also supports itinerary planning and helps users continue organizing their trip after they have selected a destination.

User Research Summary

Our user research focused on understanding how college students plan trips, what challenges they face, and what kind of support they expect from a travel planning system. We conducted semi-structured interviews with six college students with different levels of travel experience and planning confidence.

The interviews were conducted on February 4, 2026 at FSU Student Union and lasted approximately 12-15 minutes each. The interviews showed that student travel planning is strongly shaped by budget, time constraints, academic schedules, group coordination, and confidence level in making travel decisions.

Key Findings
  • Students often switch between multiple platforms to compare flights, hotels, and transportation options
  • Group travel decisions take too long because of delayed responses, different budgets, and conflicting preferences
  • Planning feels mentally tiring, especially for students with limited time, low travel experience, or busy academic schedules
What Users Need
  • A single place to compare affordable travel options
  • Clearer time and cost information
  • More guidance when planning unfamiliar trips
  • Easier ways to coordinate with friends
  • Simpler steps that reduce stress and planning effort

How GlobeTrotter Responds

GlobeTrotter was designed to respond directly to these findings. The system supports travel comparison, cost-based filtering, plan evaluation, group coordination, and itinerary management in one place. These features were chosen to reduce platform switching, support better decisions, and make trip planning more manageable for students.

Moderate Task – Comparing Travel Options
