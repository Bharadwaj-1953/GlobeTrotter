Project Overview

GlobeTrotter is a travel planning platform designed for college students. It helps users search, compare, and plan trips based on budget and preferences without switching between multiple platforms. The system also supports group coordination and itinerary planning.

User Research

Our research focused on understanding how college students plan trips and the challenges they face. We found that students often switch between multiple platforms to compare flights, hotels, and activities, which makes the process time-consuming and confusing.

Group travel decisions are difficult due to different preferences, budgets, and delayed responses. Many users also feel overwhelmed when trying to balance cost, time, and travel options.

GlobeTrotter addresses these challenges by providing a single platform that simplifies comparison, supports better decision-making, and reduces planning effort.

Storyboards

Simple Task – Budget Trip Planning

A student searches for travel options within a fixed budget, compares available plans, and saves the best option.

Moderate Task – Comparing Travel Options

Users compare multiple travel options and evaluate trade-offs between cost and travel time.

Complex Task – Advanced Travel Planning

Users explore travel options and make decisions using multiple factors such as budget, duration, and preferences.
