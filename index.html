Welcome to GlobeTrotter

🎯

Our Mission

GlobeTrotter is a student-centered travel planning platform designed to make trip planning easier for college students. We bring important planning steps into one unified place so users don't need to switch between multiple websites.

🌍

What We Do

We help users compare travel options, manage budgets, reduce planning stress, and coordinate decisions easily when traveling alone or with a group. Our platform supports flights, hotels, activities, and complete itinerary management.

💡

The Problem

Students struggle switching between multiple platforms, face complex group coordination, and experience planning fatigue. GlobeTrotter solves these issues with unified comparison, structured voting, and simplified workflows.

User Research

We conducted semi-structured interviews with 6 college students on February 4, 2026 at FSU Student Union. Each session lasted 12-15 minutes and explored how students plan trips, what challenges they face, and what support they expect.

⚠

Key Problems

  • Switching between multiple platforms causes frustration
  • Group decisions take too long with delayed responses
  • Different budgets and preferences create conflict
  • Planning feels mentally exhausting and overwhelming
  • Information overload makes decisions difficult
💭

Key Insights

  • Budget is the primary decision factor for all students
  • Time constraints heavily influence planning behavior
  • Less experienced travelers need guidance and reassurance
  • Group travel adds significant complexity to planning
  • Students value simple, streamlined planning tools
✓

User Needs

  • Single place to compare affordable travel options
  • Clear cost and time information for decisions
  • Guidance for unfamiliar or complex trips
  • Easy group coordination and budget alignment
  • Simplified steps that reduce planning stress

How GlobeTrotter Responds

GlobeTrotter was designed to directly address these findings. The system supports travel comparison, cost-based filtering, plan evaluation, group coordination, and itinerary management in one unified place. By reducing platform switching, supporting better decisions, and simplifying planning workflows, GlobeTrotter makes trip planning genuinely manageable for students.

How It Works

Simple Task

Budget Trip Planning

A student searches for travel options within a fixed budget, compares available plans, and saves the option that best fits their needs.

Moderate Task

Comparing Travel Options

Users compare multiple travel choices and evaluate trade-offs between cost and travel time before making a decision.

Complex Task

Advanced Travel Planning

Users explore detailed travel choices and make decisions using multiple factors such as budget, duration, and preferences.
