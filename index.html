About the Project

GlobeTrotter is a travel planning system designed for college students. The goal of the system is to simplify how students search, compare, and plan trips without switching between multiple platforms.

The system allows users to explore travel options based on budget, preferences, and constraints, compare plans, and make informed decisions. It also supports group planning and itinerary management.

User Research

We conducted semi-structured interviews with six college students to understand how they currently plan trips and the challenges they face.

Students reported that planning requires switching between multiple platforms such as airline websites and booking apps. This makes the process time-consuming and difficult when comparing options. Group travel adds additional complexity when balancing preferences and budgets.

Key Problems

  • Switching between multiple platforms
  • Slow group decision-making
  • Difficulty comparing cost and time
  • Planning feels overwhelming

User Needs

  • Compare options in one place
  • Clear cost and time information
  • Support for group planning
  • Simple workflows
